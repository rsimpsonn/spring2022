Contact Us

If you have questions, you should post on Edstem or come to our office hours. As a general rule, use Edstem rather than email, as that helps us manage communications among the staff. Edstem allows private posts that will only be seen by the poster and the staff. Of course, if your question is of a personal nature that should only be directed to the instructors or the HTAs, feel free to email us directly. (The syllabus contains more detailed guidelines on who to contact for what.)

Emailing milda_zizyte@brown.edu will reach Milda, cs0111headtas@cs.brown.edu will reach the HTAs, and cs0111tas@cs.brown.edu will reach all of the staff. Please mail the entire TA list only if you aren't able to post on EdStem.
